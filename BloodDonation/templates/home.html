Workflow-based web-enabled service-centric comprehensive IT solution for managing processes and services of an individual Blood Bank or Blood Bank attached to a Hospital.

SaaS solution is based on a multi-tenant architecture and made available over Cloud Infrastructure. With this model, a single version of the application, with a single configuration (hardware, network, operating system), is proposed to be used by multiple Blood Banks ("tenants") across various states.

Web App

Web based Blood Bank Management System on open source platform.

Mobile App

Android Mobile app for citizen as well as BloodBank available in Play Store.

SBTM(State Blood Transfusion Monitoring Application)

All Blood banks of a State are connected with State Blood Transfusion Monitoring Application ,module of e-BloodBank Application designed to Monitor and better manage Blood Bank Operation across State.




Available Modules

e-Bloodbank@NIC Web Application have all the necessary module for a Standard Blood bank.

Donor Management

Pre-screening of Applicant,Checking Registration parametes,Capturing Vital parametes,Capture Applicant Blood Group(From Analyser)

Bleeding Entry & Screening

Donor Bleeding Parameter Capture, TTI Screening,Adverse Reaction Details Entry.

Blood Transfusion

Transfusion Request from HMIS,Verification of Blood Sample,Crossmatching,Issue of Blood,Transfusion Reaction Entry.

Blood Camp Management

Camp Registration,Allocation Of No,Camp Donor Entry,Generate e-Donor Card.

Receive & Transfer of Blood

Transfer of Stock Between mother Bloodbank and Storage Center,Receive Stock,Detailed MIS.

Aphearisis

Donor Selection,Prepare Aphearisis Component,Quality Check,Comprehensive MIS.
