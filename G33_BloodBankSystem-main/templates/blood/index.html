Donate Blood, Save Lives

Blood donation is a simple yet powerful way to help your community. Each drop you give brings hope to someone in need — from accident victims to patients with chronic diseases.

Why Donate Blood?

Every year, millions rely on blood transfusions to survive. Whether it's for surgery, trauma recovery, or disease treatment — there's no substitute for donated blood. When you donate, you're offering someone a second chance at life.

Before You Donate

  • Get a good night's sleep the day before.
  • Eat a healthy meal and drink water before donating.
  • Bring an ID and avoid heavy physical activity post-donation.

Who Can Donate?

  • You must be in good general health.
  • Age: 17+ (16 with parental consent).
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • No recent infections or surgeries.
